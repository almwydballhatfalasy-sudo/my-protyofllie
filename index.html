Executive Profile

Dr. Maged Atef El-Essi stands as a distinguished architect of modern Arab diplomacy, specializing in the intersection of youth empowerment and global policy. His career is marked by a relentless pursuit of cross-cultural excellence and the elevation of young innovators on the world stage.

As the Secretary General of the Arab Youth Union for Creativity and Innovation, he has pioneered regional frameworks that bridge the gap between traditional governance and the dynamic potential of the digital age. His work facilitates dialogue between international institutions and the next generation of Arab leaders.
